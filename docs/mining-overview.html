Mining Overview v1.0

Retinecy Network Coin (RTNC)
Status: Public Documentation • Mining: Not Live (prep phase)
Important: RTNC does not promote speculation, investment, or financial returns. This page explains mining as a technical participation and network verification process.

What Mining Is (In RTNC)

Mining is the process that proposes and validates blocks in a proof-of-work network. In RTNC, mining exists to demonstrate a transparent, publicly verifiable rule-set for network operation and issuance.

What Mining Is Not

  • Mining is not presented as an income opportunity or a promise of financial return.
  • RTNC does not publish or endorse profitability claims, yield claims, or “earnings” messaging.
  • There is no privileged access, private allocation, or insider mining program.
  • No one receives special mining parameters, early windows, or private launch advantages.

Network Participation Goals

RTNC’s mining design is intended to support:

  • Open participation: anyone can run a node and validate the chain.
  • Public verification: rules, documentation, and audit trails are accessible.
  • Abuse resistance: design choices are reviewed publicly and documented.
  • Operational transparency: status, changes, and announcements are recorded.

Phased Participation Plan

RTNC is documented as a multi-phase proof-of-work network. Phases are intended to be announced publicly with clear rules and notice windows before activation.

Phase 1: CPU Phase 2: GPU Phase 3: ASIC
Public Notice Rule: Any activation of a phase must be announced publicly in advance, with a written change-log and published documentation, so participation conditions are visible to all.

Fairness & No-Early-Advantage Commitments

  • No premine: RTNC does not distribute coins privately before public participation.
  • No private allocations: no VC allocation, vesting pool, or insider tranche.
  • Public rules: emission and validation rules are documented and verifiable.
  • Community review: documentation and changes are open to public scrutiny.

Current Status

As of this document version, RTNC mining is in a preparation phase:

  • Documentation and public transparency materials are being finalized.
  • Test environments (testnet/regtest) may be used for development and validation.
  • Mainnet mining is not active until public launch rules are finalized and published.
