Why RTNC Exists

RTNC is designed to demonstrate that a digital asset can operate transparently, predictably, and fairly without insider allocation, financial engineering, or speculative mechanics.

Core Principles

Open Participation

Anyone can mine, run a node, or inspect the chain.

No Gates

No premine, no insider allocations, no privileged access.

Transparency

Emission, activity, and governance rules are fully visible.

Neutrality

RTNC does not promise returns or promote speculation.
